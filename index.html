Gavan Wilhite

Technologist & Storyteller

linkedin

Gavan Wilhite is currently a Founder and Director of Engineering at AltspaceVR, a social VR company enabling people to do things together regardless of distance. He has spent his career leveraging game design and technology to impact other fields. Prior to his work with VR, he was at The Walt Disney Company exploring new ways to educate children using the principles of game design. He enjoys exploring how causes such as effective altruism can be furthered through the use of emerging technologies.
